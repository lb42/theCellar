Current state of P5

This is a working document for the use of the TEI Council and Editors. It summarizes briefly the current state of play and intended future work with respect to each chapter of P5. For more detailed information, consult the working directory currently maintained in the Perforce repository at //TEI/P5/ but which it is proposed to move to Sourceforge in the short to medium term. For strategic information about the overall goals of the P5 revision, and the version of it currently visible to the public, see http://www.tei-c.org/P5.

This document lists the chapters of P5, with an indication of the current status of each

Under the heading ‘prognosis’, we give our current assessment of the amount of work needed on each chapter, defined in the following broad terms:

stet
No action needed
major surgery
Complete rewrite needed
minor surgery
Basically sound, but new material needs to be added
for review
New material has been provided but not yet approved by the council
for checking
No major changes anticipated but not yet checked for P5 consistency
for deletion
Proposed for removal at P5

The process of ‘checking for P5 consistency’ will involve a number of minor editorial checks and changes which, although pervasive, cannot be done automatically. (Pervasive but automatable changes are applied across P5 as and when they are identified). This document also summarizes the tasks so far identified as necessary in this respect.

The status ‘Unchanged from P4’ is not strictly accurate: all chapters of P5 have gone through a number of global syntactic changes, and will go through more before we are done.

The prognoses given in the following table are highly provisional! In particular, we don't yet know what effect the promised user consultation exercise (see edw78.html) may have on them.

Provisional Workplan

The following involve major work on a chapter. They are all urgent:

  1. Expand discussion of customization features in TD and make sure it works: [LB by 31 Jul]
  2. Revise MD, CF, and IN in light of new customization features. Document features for modification and i18n [? after TD]
  3. Expand SG to include discussion of RelaxNG and other schemas [urgent]: mostly needs prose tidy-up, and some relaxng schema examples: needed for Fabio's book: [SB by 31 Jul]
  4. Revise ST: in particular
    • overhaul discussion of class system
    • add new material about proposed new core modules
    [LB by 31 Jul]
  5. Review HD and update its metadata discussion: consolidate and move discussion of other metadata schemes in current HD and IH; review other schemes not so far discussed; create new chapter about compatibility and mapping, to include (possibly) discussion about using namespaces, using existing MD methods, using generic metadata tags (if so proposed by teilib)
  6. Consider reorganization/modularization of Core [urgent]
  7. Write up implementn of <choice> mechanism [urgent]: a draft proposal is needed to build on ideas from tei-choice
  8. Add discussion of prosopographic issues to ND, moving it fron MS and CO [urgent]: a draft proposal is needed
  9. Revise DI: waiting on LR, due end Jul
  10. Revise TE: waiting on LR, due end Jul
  11. Revise MS: waiting on MS-WG: due 15 jul, then to LB
  12. Review FD for compatibility with new FS, pending ISO revision: draft shd be ready for paris, end aug.
  13. Implement proposals from SA. Specifically:
    • Add UR[ILN] attribute to <ptr> and <ref>; remove xref and xptr
    • Revise discussion of syntax for extended pointers xpaths
    • Add discussion of standoff annotation to NH (or SA?)
    (SB by end Jul?)

The following can be done at any time: for revised chapters, they will be done during revision; for others, they will be done during final sweep through (c. sept).

  1. Check that none of the typos etc. lately fixed in P4 (as documented in edw77.xml) has survived into P5 [passim]
  2. Check content model of all <egXML> elements still containing CDATA marked sections and remove same, if syntactically possible [passim]
  3. Check and amend references to DTD language constructs [passim]
  4. Check and amend references to P4 structural architecture (eg base/additional tagsets) [passim]
  5. Implement renaming of id to xml:id [passim]
  6. Implement renaming of lang to xml:lang [passim]
  7. Revise base chapters to remove references to their status as bases

The following are either non-urgent or undecided:

  1. Check conformity of CH language identification recs with eg Ethnolog practice [soon]
  2. Consider what to do about remaining CDATA attributes [passim]
  3. Merge CF and IN into new freestanding document on Conformance
  4. Decide whether or not to kill GD and do it
  5. Decide whether or not to split up FT; bring it up to date
  6. Decide what to do with CR and do it

The following cannot be done until everything else is finished:

  1. Draft new preface to reflect current state of affairs. [final]
  2. Rewrite AB to reflect current state of affairs. [final]
  3. Either update or remove BIB

Drafts will go through the following stages:

  • an editor or a workgroup has produced the draft and it has been seen by the council
  • both editors have worked on the chapter; council has approved its content
  • the chapter has been available for public comment for at least a month

Substantative changes to intellectual content should not be made once a chapter has become `stable'
