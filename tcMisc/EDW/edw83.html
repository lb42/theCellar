Summary

This document lists all materials for consideration as changes to TEI P5 submitted to the TEI Council as a result of the general consultancy exercise launched with the announcement of TEI P5 in Spring 2004. It includes :

  • specific (but not always substantiated) feature change requests submitted to the TEI SourceForge site, as of 31 Aug 2004
  • drafts for complete or partial new chapters prepared by TEI Work Groups or other agencies
  • proposals from TEI SIGs or other agencies made in postings to TEI discussion lists, etc.
